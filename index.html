🔄 Recovery Plan

⚠️ Stop the Bleeding

• Reduce to 0.5% risk per trade
• Take 2-3 day break after big loss
• Never increase risk to "make it back"
• Review last 10 trades for patterns

🎯 3-Step Recovery

Step 1: Demo trade 1 week
Step 2: Micro lots (0.01) until consistent
Step 3: Gradually increase size

💪 Mental Reset

• Accept loss as part of trading
• Focus on process, not money
• Set small daily goals
• Celebrate small wins
